Rock, Paper and Scissors

Rock, Paper and Scissors is a very simple game played between two people and using only their hands. The players choose among "Rock", "Paper" or "Scissors" and, at the same type, show each other what option they chose using their hands to make the format of their option.

There are three possibilities for a player: win, loss or draw. The rules are:

  • Rock crushes Scissors (Rock wins);
  • Paper covers Rock (Paper wins);
  • Scissors cuts Paper (Scissors wins);
  • Equal hands means a draw.
